5. Typography üî§

Ubuntu - Titles and Headers

This font will be used for all titles (h1, h2, h3), navigation menus, and elements that need emphasis. Ubuntu is a modern sans-serif font, readable, and full of personality.

Roboto - Body Text and Paragraphs

Roboto will be used for all body text, descriptions, paragraphs, and general content. It is highly readable on screens and pairs perfectly with Ubuntu.

Justification: The combination of Ubuntu and Roboto creates an appropriate visual contrast between titles and content, maintaining a professional and technological aesthetic ideal for a web curriculum.
